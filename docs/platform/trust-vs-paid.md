# Trust vs Paid Features

Understanding the difference between community-driven trust signals and paid promotional features.

---

## The Core Principle

{% hint style="success" %}
**Trust cannot be bought.** A project's trust score is 100% community-driven and based on real votes from verified users. No amount of money can change a trust score.
{% endhint %}

---

## Trust Features (Free & Community-Driven)

These features are earned through community engagement and cannot be purchased:

### Trust Score

The percentage displayed on every project represents genuine community sentiment:

- Calculated from weighted votes of all users
- Higher reputation users have more influence
- Older accounts carry more weight
- Updated in real-time as votes come in

**A 95% trust score means the community trusts the project. A 15% score means they don't. Simple.**

### Trust Badges

| Badge | Meaning | How Earned |
|-------|---------|------------|
| Trusted | Community approves | 70%+ score, 10+ votes |
| Neutral | Mixed opinions | 40-69% score |
| Suspicious | Proceed with caution | 20-39% score |
| Suspected Scam | Community warns against | <20% score |

### Verified Ownership

When a project team proves they control the project:

- Adds credibility (they're not hiding)
- Enables them to respond to comments
- Does NOT affect trust score
- Free to verify

---

## Paid Features (Premium Listings)

These features provide visibility but do NOT influence trust:

### Premium Badge

| What It Is | What It's NOT |
|------------|---------------|
| Shows project paid for visibility | An endorsement by DappScore |
| Indicates serious team investment | A guarantee of legitimacy |
| Unlocks promotional features | A factor in trust calculations |

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Premium ≠ Trusted**

A project can be Premium (paid) but still have a low trust score. Always check the community trust score, not just whether something is "Premium."
{% endhint %}

### Premium Benefits

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Featured Placement | Appears at top of listings |
| Premium Badge | Gold badge on listing |
| Priority Support | Faster response from team |
| Analytics Dashboard | See who's viewing your project |

### What Premium Does NOT Do

- Does NOT boost trust score
- Does NOT hide negative comments
- Does NOT prevent downvotes
- Does NOT guarantee approval

---

## Visual Indicators

### How to Spot the Difference

**Trust Indicators (Community-Driven):**
- Green/Red trust percentage (e.g., "87% Trust")
- Trust level badge (Trusted, Suspicious, etc.)
- Vote count
- Comment sentiment

**Paid Indicators (Premium):**
- Gold "Premium" badge
- "Featured" label at top of lists
- Premium border/styling

---

## Why This Matters

### For Users

You can trust that:
- Trust scores reflect real community opinion
- Premium projects aren't being "pushed" as trustworthy
- Your votes genuinely matter
- The platform isn't pay-to-win

### For Projects

You should understand:
- Premium gets you visibility, not credibility
- Community trust must be earned
- A good product + transparency = good trust score
- Paying won't save a bad project

## FAQ

<details>
<summary><strong>Can a project pay to remove bad reviews?</strong></summary>

No. Comments and votes cannot be removed by paying. Only spam or Terms violations are removed by admins.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Does Premium affect search rankings?</strong></summary>

Premium projects appear first in "Featured" sections, but standard search results are sorted by trust score and activity, not payment status.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if a Premium project is a scam?</strong></summary>

The community can still downvote it. Premium status does not protect against low trust scores. If a Premium project gets flagged as a scam, it may be removed and forfeit their payment.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Is there any way to buy trust?</strong></summary>

No. Trust scores are calculated purely from community votes. The only way to improve trust is to build a legitimate project and earn community confidence.
</details>

## Summary

| Aspect | Trust Features | Paid Features |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| How obtained | Community votes | Payment |
| Can be bought | No | Yes |
| Indicates legitimacy | Yes | No |
| Affects visibility | Through high scores | Through placement |
| What to trust | Always | Never alone |

{% hint style="info" %}
**Bottom Line:** Look at the trust score, read the comments, check the votes. Premium is just marketing - trust is what matters.
{% endhint %}
