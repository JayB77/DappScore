## Adding to Wallet

To add $SCORE to MetaMask or other wallets:

1. Open your wallet
2. Click "Import Token" or "Add Token"
3. Select "Custom Token"
4. Enter the contract address (see above)
5. Token details will auto-populate
6. Click "Add"
