## How to Buy

<details>
<summary><strong>Step-by-step guide</strong></summary>

1. **Visit** [dappscore.io/token-sale](https://dappscore.io/token-sale)
2. **Connect** your wallet (MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, etc.)
3. **Switch** to Base network if prompted
4. **Select** your payment method (ETH, USDC, or USDT)
5. **Enter** the amount you want to spend
6. **Confirm** the transaction in your wallet
7. **Wait** for the sale to end
8. **Claim** your tokens when claiming is enabled!

## Claim Process

{% hint style="info" %}
**Claim-Based Distribution** - Tokens are held in the contract during the sale and distributed after it ends. This ensures fair distribution and prevents manipulation.
{% endhint %}

After the sale ends:

1. Owner enables claiming
2. Visit the token sale page
3. Click "Claim Tokens"
4. Confirm the transaction
5. Receive your $SCORE tokens!
