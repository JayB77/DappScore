## Vesting Schedules

### Team & Advisors
- 1 year cliff (no tokens released)
- 3 year linear vesting after cliff
- Total: 4 years until fully vested

### Community Rewards
- Distributed over 4 years
- Released based on platform activity
- Emission rate decreases over time

- Portion of protocol fees used for buyback and burn
- Governance can vote to adjust burn rate
- Maximum burn capped at 50% of total supply
