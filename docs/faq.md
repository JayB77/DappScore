# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is DappScore?
DappScore is a community-driven platform for vetting crypto projects. Users vote, comment, and earn $SCORE tokens for helping identify trustworthy projects.

### What blockchain does DappScore use?
DappScore is built on Base (Layer 2 on Ethereum). Currently live on Base Sepolia testnet, with mainnet launch coming soon.

### Is DappScore free to use?
Yes! Browsing, voting, and commenting are completely free. Premium listings cost 100 USDC.

## Wallet & Connection

### Which wallets are supported?
Any WalletConnect-compatible wallet, including:
- MetaMask
- Coinbase Wallet
- Rainbow
- Trust Wallet
- And many more

### Do I need ETH to use DappScore?
Yes, you need a small amount of ETH on Base for gas fees when voting or commenting. Premium listings require USDC.

### How do I switch to Base network?
When you connect your wallet, you'll be prompted to switch. Or manually add:
- Network Name: Base
- RPC URL: https://mainnet.base.org
- Chain ID: 8453
- Symbol: ETH

## Voting & Reputation

### How does voting work?
Connect your wallet, navigate to a project, and click Trust (upvote) or Distrust (downvote). Your vote is recorded on-chain.

### What determines vote weight?
Your reputation level. Higher reputation = more voting power (up to 3x for Trusted users).

### Can I change my vote?
Not currently. Votes are permanent once submitted.

### How do I increase my reputation?
Vote on projects, write quality comments that get upvoted, and report actual scams.

## Projects

### How do I submit a project?
Click "Submit Project", fill out the form, choose Free or Premium, and submit. See [Submitting Projects](platform/submitting-projects.md).

### Can I edit my project after submission?
Yes, if you verified ownership. Click "Edit Project" on your project page.

### What's the difference between Free and Premium?
Premium listings (100 USDC) are featured at the top for 7 days with a special badge. Free listings rely on trust score for positioning.

### How is trust score calculated?
Based on weighted votes from the community. Higher reputation voters have more influence.

## $SCORE Token

### What is $SCORE used for?
Governance, staking rewards, premium features, and earning through platform participation.

### How do I earn $SCORE?
Vote on projects, write helpful comments, report scams, and stake tokens.

### Where can I buy $SCORE?
During the token sale at dappscore.io/token-sale, and on DEXs after launch.

### Is there a max supply?
Yes, 100,000,000 $SCORE maximum.
